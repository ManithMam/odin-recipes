Fried Rice

Description

This is a recipe for a simple and easy fried rice.

Ingridients

  • Rice(Best is 1 day old rice)
  • Protein
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Use your 1 day old rice or cook your rice if you do not have rice already.
  2. Cook the Protein in a frying pan.
  3. After the Protein is done add the vegetables.
  4. Add salt pepper and other spicing you like.
  5. Add rice in the pan.
  6. Toss around.
  7. Add soy sauce.
  8. Create a hole in the middle and add 2 eggs.
  9. Stir the ggs and mix with the rice until cooked through./li>
