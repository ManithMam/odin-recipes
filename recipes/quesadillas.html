Quessadillas

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious and simple quessadilla.

Ingridients

  • Tortillas
  • A protein
  • Shredded cheese
  • Vegetables
  • Cooking oil: Olive oil etc.

Steps

  1. Prepare the filling. Put your oil in a pan.
  2. Cook the protein first until halfway through.
  3. Add the vegetables.
  4. Cook both until done.
  5. Remove the filling from the pan to a seperate tray.
  6. Put the tortilla in the pan.
  7. Put cheese in the tortilla and add the filling.
  8. Fold it in half.
  9. Cook it on both sides until golden brown.
  10. If it is done slice it up into triangles.
