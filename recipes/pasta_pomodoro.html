Pasta Pomodoro

Description

This is a recipe for a pasta with tomatoe sauce.

Ingridients

  • Pasta
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • salt, pepper etc.
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a salted pot of water.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, cut the onion and garlic.
  3. Cut cherry tomatoes in half.
  4. Cook the onion and garlic in a pan with oliv oil.
  5. Once the onion is done add the cherry tomatoes.
  6. Cook them until all the juices are out. Crush them to speed up the process.
  7. Add basil leaves to the sauce.
  8. Once 8 min are over add pasta water and the pasta to the pan.
  9. Cook for 2 minutes.
